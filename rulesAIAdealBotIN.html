Rules for Uploading Works and Participating in the Contest

The contest will take place from May 1 to May 31, 2025

Hello, dear user of neural networks and Telegram. These rules describe the instructions and public agreement for participation in our contest, with the prize being a monthly subscription to ChatGPT Plus! These rules may change, and each participant will be notified in advance.

In brief: To participate in the subscription contest, upload 1 to 7 works (images in jpeg, png, or videos in mp4 up to 3 seconds) from public AIs (with public links to the generation) to the bot by May 20, 2025, and complete 1 or more sets (10 pairs of works) for testing from May 1 to May 31, 2025. The more works and sets, the more chances you have. Good luck!

Questions of research integrity and test security:

1. The time for selecting works is limited to 15 seconds. During the draw from May 1 to May 31, there will be less time for selection. The exact amount of time will be announced before the start of the draw.

2. The bot cannot be restarted, but it may automatically restart during a test if there's a poor internet connection. The selection time does not stop during such a restart.

Questions and tech support in Telegram @Gordaniele

1. Uploading and Contesting Works

1.1. You can upload up to 7 works — images in jpg (jpeg), png, and videos in mp4 up to 3 seconds, preferably with horizontal resolution — by May 20, 2025, inclusive.

1.2. Each work you upload must be generated exclusively by a public neural network.

1.3. The work must be free from manual edits in graphic editors. We all want to see what neural networks can do.

1.4. Works can be sent compressed or as files. Add a public link to the generation (mandatory) with the work. The neural network's name, your contact information, and descriptions are welcome!

1.5. Works are checked manually since no AI is currently able to distinguish between real works and generated ones.

1.6. Approved works — the number of works and the owner — will be posted on the Telegram channel Ideal AI the day after submission.

1.7. Approved works submitted before May 20 will be part of the contest in May 2025.

1.8. The contest will run from May 1 to May 31, 2025. Works can be added until May 20, 2025, inclusive. Works submitted after May 20 will participate in the June 2025 contest.

1.9. Uploading images or videos is considered an application for participation in the contest.

1.10. If none of your works are approved, we will notify you and suggest submitting new ones.

1.11. Each work participates in the contest once per month.

1.12. Works are added to the bot by us in sets of 10 works and 10 real photos (10 pairs for selection). This will be announced immediately on the Telegram channel Ideal AI.

1.13. Works in a set are shuffled randomly, so images may be compared to videos.

1.14. The work that is selected as real most often by 11:59 PM on May 31, 2025, will win.

2. Participation in the Main Contest

2.1. To participate in the main contest, where the winner will receive a monthly subscription to ChatGPT Plus, a Telegram user must fulfill all the conditions outlined in these rules.

2.1.1. To participate in the main contest, the Telegram user must upload from 1 (one) to 7 (seven) works to the bot by May 20 and complete at least 1 (one) set of testing from May 1 to May 31.

2.2. Participation in the test contest is achieved by clicking the "Start Test" button in the bot from May 1 to May 31 and selecting works.

2.3. After starting the test, each participant receives two pictures or videos with buttons. The correct answer (+1 point) is the work that was NOT generated by artificial intelligence.

2.4. The participant must compare the works themselves and click the button under the one that they believe was NOT generated by AI.

2.5. For each correct answer, the participant receives 1 point. After each choice, a message will appear below the pictures indicating whether the choice was correct.

2.6. After completing 10 pairs of works, the participant will receive their result for the set and can continue the test by pressing the "Continue" button.

2.7. The works in each set of 10 pairs are randomly chosen, so images may be compared to videos.

2.8. Sets of works are added to the bot by us during the contest until May 21, 2025. A Telegram user can test each set only once.

2.9. The participant with the most points at the end of the month will be declared the winner of the test contest.

3. Winning and Conditions

3.1. Using obvious bugs in the bot's work, as well as uploading real images and videos, will be detected and considered by us as unfair participation in the contest.

3.2. Googling works will reduce the chances of your pictures winning, so play fairly. According to AI, the chance of guessing correctly in any binomial distribution is no more than 20%. However, we decided to assume it's 50%.

3.3. The winner will be announced on June 1, 2025, based on the results of both contests: the test contest and the works contest. The announcement will be made on the Ideal AI channel.

3.4. The winner will receive a monthly subscription to ChatGPT Plus.

3.5. It will be fun!
